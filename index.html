What This Tool Does

This AI tool analyzes a live image of your plant and tells you its health condition. It can detect: Healthy, Water Issues, Light Issues, Nitrogen Deficiency, Potassium Deficiency, and Phosphorus Deficiency. This tool can help gardeners, beginners, and plant owners quickly diagnose common plant problems.

How to Use the Plant Health Detector

  1. Place your plant in front of your webcam.
  2. Press the green Start button below.
  3. Hold the plant steady and let the AI analyze it.
  4. Read the live predictions that appear underneath the video.
